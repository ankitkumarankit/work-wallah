Online Reputation Manager

Location: Remote / Delhi NCR

Type: Full-time | Experience: 2+ Years

We are seeking a proactive and detail-oriented Online Reputation Manager to monitor, manage, and enhance our brand's image across digital platforms. You’ll respond to reviews, manage crisis communication, and build positive brand sentiment.

Requirements:
  • 2+ years of experience in brand or reputation management
  • Excellent communication and crisis-handling skills
  • Experience with ORM tools (Google Alerts, Mention, Brandwatch, etc.)
