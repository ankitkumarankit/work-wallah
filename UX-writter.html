UX Writer

Location: Remote / India

Type: Full-time | Experience: 2+ Years

As a UX Writer, you'll collaborate with designers and product teams to create helpful, concise, and engaging copy for our products. You'll shape user experience through thoughtful content and drive clarity across every interaction.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Write clear, concise microcopy for apps and interfaces
  • Align tone and language with brand voice
  • Collaborate with UX designers and product managers
  • Conduct UX research and content testing
Required Skills:
  • Strong writing/editing skills with UX understanding
  • Familiarity with design tools (Figma, Adobe XD)
  • Experience in tech or digital products preferred
